## COPY NOTES

**Tone:** Direct. Peer-to-peer. No corporate speak. Short paragraphs. Never more than 200 words per email.

**Subject line rules:**
- Use numbers when possible ("5 spots", "6 hours a week")
- Use quotes to handle objections ("I'm not technical enough")
- Never clickbait — deliver on the subject line immediately

**CTA rules:**
- One CTA per email. Always.
- Calendly link OR reply — never both in the same email
- Make the CTA a full sentence, not just "Click here"

**Personalization tokens to add (via your email platform):**
- {{first_name}} — add to all subject lines and openers once you collect names
- {{company_name}} — useful for B2B targeting later
